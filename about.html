About Vidyasagar

Empowering students with practical skills and knowledge for over 15 years. Discover our mission, vision, and the team behind Vidyasagar.

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by Dr. Rajesh Vidyasagar, our institute began with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional education and industry requirements. What started as a small training center with just two courses has now evolved into one of India's leading skill development institutes.

Over the years, we have helped thousands of students transform their careers through our industry-relevant curriculum and practical training approach. Our alumni network spans across major cities in India and abroad, with many holding leadership positions in prestigious organizations.

Today, Vidyasagar Skills Development Institute stands as a testament to our commitment to quality education and student success. We continue to evolve our programs to meet the changing demands of the global job market.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive everything we do at Vidyasagar.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with industry-relevant skills and knowledge that enhance their employability and career growth in an ever-evolving job market.
Provide accessible, high-quality education to students from diverse backgrounds
Foster an environment of practical learning and innovation
Bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements

Our Vision

To be recognized as India's premier skill development institute, known for excellence in education, innovation in teaching methodologies, and successful career outcomes for our students
Establish centers of excellence across major cities in India
Continuously evolve our curriculum to stay ahead of industry trends
Create a global network of skilled professionals who contribute to their fields
